Safety First

Never train in water, driving, or standing. Always practice lying on a bed or sofa, in a safe environment with no risk of falling.

This app is a training aid, not a medical device. Always consult with a healthcare professional if you have any concerns.

Start gradually and listen to your body. Stop immediately if you feel dizzy, lightheaded, or unwell.
