Privacy Policy for Aegean Breath

Effective Date: November 24, 2025

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Aegean Breath ("the App", "we", "our") handles your information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Aegean Breath is a breath-holding training application designed for freediving and apnea enthusiasts. The App helps you track your training progress, create custom breathing tables, and monitor your performance.

Our Privacy Commitment

We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data with third parties.

All data generated by your use of the App is stored locally on your device and remains under your complete control.

Information Storage and Use

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

1. Training Data

  • Breathing Tables: Custom CO2 tolerance tables, O2 adaptation tables, and user-defined training routines including preparation times, hold durations, number of rounds, and increment values
  • Session History: Records of your training sessions including timestamps, session duration, session type, completion status, and optional personal notes
  • Performance Metrics: Contraction timestamps during breath holds and your maximum breath-hold achievements
  • Heart Rate Data: Heart rate measurements collected from connected Bluetooth devices during training sessions

2. App Preferences

  • Display Settings: Your chosen visual theme and appearance preferences
  • Audio/Haptic Settings: Sound and vibration notification preferences
  • Training Preferences: Settings such as 5-second increment options for table configuration
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have completed the initial app introduction

3. Device Connection Information

  • Bluetooth Device Details: Saved Bluetooth device addresses and names of heart rate monitors you have paired with the App for automatic reconnection

Purpose of Data Collection

All data collected serves the following purposes:

  • Tracking your breath-holding training progress over time
  • Providing personalized training recommendations based on your performance
  • Customizing your training experience with saved preferences
  • Connecting to your heart rate monitor for health monitoring during training
  • Improving your training effectiveness through historical data analysis

Permissions Requested

The App requests the following device permissions:

Bluetooth Permissions

  • Purpose: To connect to and receive heart rate data from compatible Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) heart rate monitors
  • Permissions Used:
    • BLUETOOTH - Basic Bluetooth functionality
    • BLUETOOTH_ADMIN - Bluetooth device management
    • BLUETOOTH_SCAN - Scanning for nearby Bluetooth devices (Android 12+)
    • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT - Connecting to paired Bluetooth devices (Android 12+)
  • Data Handling: Heart rate data received from connected devices is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere

Location Permission (Android 10 and Earlier Only)

  • Permission Used: ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION (only on Android 10 and below)
  • Purpose: Required by Android's operating system to scan for Bluetooth devices on older Android versions
  • Important Note: The App does NOT collect, store, or use your location data. This permission is solely required by the Android OS for Bluetooth scanning on older devices and is explicitly flagged as "neverForLocation" on Android 12+

Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored exclusively on your device using Android's secure Room database and DataStore systems
  • No Cloud Transmission: The App does not connect to the internet or transmit any data to external servers
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools
  • No Third-Party Services: The App does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services
  • Device Backup: The App supports Android's built-in backup system, allowing you to restore your data if you switch devices (controlled through your device's backup settings)

Data Retention and Deletion

  • User Control: You have complete control over all data stored by the App
  • Manual Deletion: You can delete individual training sessions or breathing tables at any time through the App's interface
  • Complete Reset: The App provides a "Reset All Data" option in settings to delete all stored information
  • Uninstallation: Uninstalling the App will remove all associated data from your device (unless backed up through Android's system backup)

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers, there are no special privacy considerations for users under the age of 13. All data remains on the user's device under the control of the device owner.

Health and Safety

The App is designed as a training tool for breath-holding practice. Please note:

  • Always practice in a safe environment (sitting or lying down)
  • Never practice breath-holding in water, while driving, or in any dangerous situation
  • The heart rate monitoring feature is for informational purposes only and should not be used as a medical device
  • Consult with a healthcare professional before beginning any breath-holding training program
  • The App and its developers assume no liability for injuries or health issues resulting from training practices

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics or crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No payment processors
  • No cloud storage services

The only external interaction is direct Bluetooth communication with heart rate monitors you explicitly choose to connect.

Your Privacy Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over:

  • Access: You can view all your training data and settings within the App
  • Modification: You can edit or update your training tables, session notes, and preferences at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual sessions, tables, or reset all data completely
  • Export: You can use Android's backup system to export your data
  • Portability: Your data can be backed up and restored to another device using Android's built-in backup mechanism

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your information to third parties for any reason. Since no data leaves your device, there are no circumstances under which your information would be shared with:

  • Advertising companies
  • Analytics providers
  • Other app developers
  • Government agencies (unless legally compelled to provide access to your physical device)
  • Any other third parties

International Users

The App functions identically for users worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers, international data transfer regulations do not apply. Your data never leaves your device except through:

  • Android's optional backup system (controlled by your device settings)
  • Your manual export of data

Open Source and Transparency

The App's code structure demonstrates our commitment to privacy:

  • No internet permission requested in the Android manifest
  • No network communication code
  • All data storage uses local Android database systems
  • No embedded tracking SDKs or analytics libraries
