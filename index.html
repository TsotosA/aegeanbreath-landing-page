Why Training with Aegean?

We combine professional freediving principles with modern technology to help you safely extend your limits.

Auto-Generated Tables

Based on your personal best, we calculate the perfect CO2 and O2 tables to push your limits safely without overtraining.

Bluetooth Heart Rate

Connect your HR monitor to see exactly how your body reacts to low oxygen. Visualize your dive reflex in real-time.

Eyes-Free Training

Guided by "Alfie", our voice assistant. Haptic vibrations and audio cues let you train with your eyes closed for maximum relaxation.

Contraction Tracking

Log when your contractions start. Analyze your tolerance phase and learn to get comfortable with the uncomfortable.

Offline & Private

Modern Interface
